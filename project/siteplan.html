WDD 131 - Site Plan Document

LinkedIn I-Learn Scriptures Final Project

Summary

The site plan document is a planning document that outlines the site's purpose, target audience, content, and other important aspects of the website. The document is used to guide the development of the website and ensure that the site meets the needs of the target audience. The site plan document is an essential part of the website development process and should be created before any design or development work begins.

Site Name

Automated Calling Logs Platform

This name represents a platform that allows users to log and track automated calls. The platform is designed to help users keep track of their automated calls and monitor their effectiveness.

Optional domain availability: automated-logs.company-domain.com

Site Purpose

The Automated Calling Logs Platform is designed to provide users with a platform to log and track automated calls. The platform allows users to create an account, log their calls, and monitor the performance of their calls. The site also provides detailed analytics and reporting on the calls, allowing users to identify areas for improvement and track the effectiveness of their calls.

Scenarios

Scenarios are questions that are asked by a site visitor who represents the target audience of the organization or company. The questions will help you drive the content of the website as it provides answers to the questions. Again, much of the content specifications will actually be provided but start thinking about the target audience of the site. Include at least two scenarios.

  • Scenario 1: A small business owner who uses automated calls to reach out to customers wants to track the effectiveness of their calls. They are looking for a platform that allows them to log their calls and monitor the results.
  • Scenario 2: A marketing professional who is responsible for managing automated call campaigns for a large organization wants to track the performance of their calls and identify areas for improvement. They are looking for a platform that provides detailed analytics and reporting on their calls.
  • Scenario 3: A customer service manager who uses automated calls to provide information to customers wants to ensure that their calls are being delivered successfully. They are looking for a platform that provides real-time monitoring of their calls and alerts them to any issues.

Color Schema

The color scheme for the Automated Calling Logs Platform is designed to convey a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness. The primary colors are:

#F3F3F3
#404446
#0A7EC3

These colors are used to create a clean and modern look for the platform, with the blue color providing a contrast to highlight important elements and calls to action. The light gray color is used for backgrounds and secondary elements, while the dark gray color is used for text and other content.

Typography

Roboto will be the primary font for the Automated Calling Logs Platform. Its versatile, modern design ensures clarity and legibility on screens of all sizes, suitable for both headings and body text. Available in various weights and styles, it offers flexibility in design and layout.
